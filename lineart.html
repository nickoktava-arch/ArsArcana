Mastering the Line: Digital Line Art

From Confident Stroke to Communicative Line Weight

Line art is the skeleton of a great illustration. It's more than tracing a sketch; it's the intentional act of using confident strokes and varied weight to convey light, shadow, depth, and material before any color is applied. Mastering this phase is the key to professional-looking digital art.

"The line should tell a story before the color even starts."
